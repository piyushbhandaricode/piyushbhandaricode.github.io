A useful incident review is short, factual, and action-oriented.

## Suggested Structure

1. Incident summary
2. Customer impact
3. Timeline (UTC)
4. Root cause and contributing factors
5. Mitigations completed
6. Follow-up actions with owners and due dates

## Common Pitfalls

- Too much narrative, not enough decisions
- Missing owners for follow-up work
- No severity-to-response mapping

If follow-up actions are not visible in planning systems, incident learning rarely turns into reliability gains.
